Services

Real-Time Object Detection

Our cutting-edge AI technology enables real-time object detection in traffic camera images. With unparalleled accuracy, we can instantly identify and classify various types of vehicles, pedestrians, and other objects on the road.

Our vehicle counting service provides valuable data for traffic analysis and planning. By automatically counting the number of vehicles passing through monitored areas, we help transportation authorities make informed decisions to optimize traffic flow and improve road safety.

We offer customizable alerts for specific vehicle types or traffic conditions. Whether it's detecting a traffic congestion, a road obstruction, or a security threat, our alerts ensure timely response and intervention to mitigate potential risks and disruptions.

In addition to our core services, we provide customized image processing solutions tailored to your specific needs. From enhancing image quality to extracting valuable insights, our team of experts is dedicated to delivering high-quality results to support your transportation initiatives.
